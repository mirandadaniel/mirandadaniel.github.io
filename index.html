judgeViz

judgeViz was my individual research project from my MSc Computer Science. judgeViz was created to explore how legal judgments can be made more accessible and easier to understand for law students. The resulting project used interactive acyclic directed graphs to break down the relationships between different judges and their opinions. judgeViz won the prize for the best graduate-level individual project in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Bristol.
